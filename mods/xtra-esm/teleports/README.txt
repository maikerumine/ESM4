teleports
=========

Mod "teleports" for minetest 4.9+ game.

Allows players to create teleports, by placing 8+1 diamond blocks on the ground.
Teleport move player who stand on it to any other nearby teleport.

rnd changes: Teleports to target teleporter within radius that best matches your viewing direction ( teleport where you go )
